Same Day Agra Tour by Superfast Train

Explore Tour

Taj Mahal day trip by train is the best way to visit the famous monument of India, Taj Mahal. When you arrive in Agra on the Indian Gatimaan express train, you can also enjoy breakfast during the train.On this day trip from Delhi to Agra, get an in-depth look at the vibrant history of the legendary Mughal Empire. Visit the famous Taj Mahal - one of India's great tributes to love, and learn about how Shah Jahan built this monument for his lifelong friend, Mumtaz Mahal. You will also visit the beautiful heritage site of Agra Fort, built in the 16th century, and our expert guides will tell you about it.

Itinerary

According to the scheduled timings of the Gatimaan express train, our driver will reach on time and pick you up from the hotel and will take you to the railway station situated in New Delhi.

Our executive will welcome you at the Agra Cantt railway station and directly will drive to visit most popular historical monuments of Agra the Taj Mahal. Enjoy beauty of the Taj Mahal and Capture some memorable photos at the Taj Mahal.

Agra fort is an historical monument worth visiting. It was the residence of the Mughal emperors and is a masterpiece of its own kind.

Lunch will take place at a 5-star hotel, most probably hotel Trident or Courtyard by Marriot hotel situated in Agra.

After having a scrumptious lunch, we move towards Itmad-ud-Daulah, or the baby taj, for a wonderful view by the river Yamuna.

Exploring the local bazaar of Agra which is full of intriguing stuff such as marble handmade items and other beautiful items that you cannot take your eyes off.

Time to head towards the Agra cantt railway station, a ride back towards home sweet home.

Arrival in Delhi and our driver will pick you up from New Delhi railway station and drop you at your respective hotel.
