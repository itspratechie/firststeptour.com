Itinerary

Arrive in Delhi, where our representative will welcome you and transfer you to your hotel. After check-in, embark on a guided tour of Old Delhi. Visit the Red Fort, Jama Masjid, and enjoy a rickshaw ride through Chandni Chowk. Later, explore India Gate and the President’s House. Dinner at a local restaurant and overnight stay in Delhi.

Explore New Delhi's highlights, including the Qutub Minar, Humayun’s Tomb, and the Lotus Temple. After lunch, visit the bustling markets of Connaught Place and enjoy some shopping. Return to your hotel for dinner and an overnight stay.

Travel to Agra after breakfast. Stop at Sikandra to see Emperor Akbar’s tomb en route. Upon arrival, check into your hotel and have lunch. Visit the Taj Mahal, followed by Agra Fort. Enjoy an evening at local markets and a traditional dinner. Overnight stay in Agra.

Depart for Jaipur, making a stop at Fatehpur Sikri. Arrive in Jaipur, check into your hotel, and enjoy lunch. Visit the City Palace, Jantar Mantar observatory, and explore the local bazaars. In the evening, experience a traditional Rajasthani dinner and cultural performance. Overnight stay in Jaipur.

Visit Amber Fort, taking an elephant ride or jeep safari to the fort. Explore Hawa Mahal and Jal Mahal. After lunch, discover Jaipur’s local handicrafts and markets. Return to your hotel for relaxation or further exploration of Jaipur’s nightlife. Overnight stay in Jaipur.

Fly to Varanasi, India’s spiritual heart. Upon arrival, check into your hotel and enjoy lunch. Visit the ghats of the Ganges, witness the evening Aarti ceremony, and experience the vibrant atmosphere of Varanasi’s ancient streets. Overnight stay in Varanasi.

Early morning boat ride on the Ganges to witness the sunrise and observe local rituals. Visit Kashi Vishwanath Temple and explore the narrow alleys of Varanasi. After lunch, transfer to the airport for your onward journey, reflecting on your diverse and enriching tour experience with First Step Tour.

This 7-day itinerary offers a deep dive into India's cultural, historical, and spiritual essence, ensuring a memorable and comprehensive travel experience.
