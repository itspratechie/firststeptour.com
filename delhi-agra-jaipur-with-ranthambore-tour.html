Delhi-Agra-Jaipur with Ranthambre Tour

Explore Tour

Relive India's legends, royal palaces and unspoiled wilderness in Ranthambore on a golden triangle tour. While the Golden Triangle Tour and Tiger Safari are popular itineraries in the beautiful cities of Delhi, Agra and Jaipur, Ranthambore is home to the Royal Bengal Tigers.

With our Golden Triangle Gallery and tiger safari, you can comb through architectural masterpieces such as palaces, forts and havelis as you see them. in the excitement of an adventurous animal safari. A view of the ethereal Taj Mahal and a visit to the beautiful Amer Fort are the highlights of the 9-day Golden Triangle and Ranthambore tour.

Itinerary

Meet our agents upon arrival in Delhi, who will transfer you to a hotel. Staying in a hotel in Delhi for the night.

Today in your Golden Triangle Tour with Ranthambore, you will be immersed in the chaos of Delhi, India’s capital city, as you embark on a full-day sightseeing tour. You will be visiting famous sights such as Raj Ghat, Shanti Vana (the cremation sites of Jawaharlal Nehru), and Jama Masjid.

Because all of these locations are in old Delhi, you will pass through the Red Fort and Chandni Chowk on your way there. You will also visit Qutub Minar, Humayun’s Tomb, India Gate (War Memorial Arch), and Lakshminarayan Temple (a modern Hindu Temple). In the evening you can go shopping in C.P and Palika bazaar. Staying in a hotel in Delhi for the night.

The third day of the Golden triangle tour with tiger safari will be spent driving to Jaipur. You will arrive in Jaipur after a five-hour journey, where our representatives will bring you to a hotel. After that, you’ll embark on a sightseeing tour of Jaipur, where you’ll see the Amber fort, Jal Mahal (Water Palce), Hawa Mahal (Wind Palace), City Palace, Jantar Mantar, The evening is free for you to do whatever you like, including shopping or visit Nahargarh fort for sunset view of Pink city jaipur, and enjoy your traditional dinner with folk dance. Staying in a hotel in Jaipur for the night.

On the day four, Complete your delicious & healthy breakfast at Hotel and Checkout from hotel and drive towards to Ranthambore National Tiger safari park. After 4 hours drive, Check-in to Hotel and Get ready by 3pm for evening tiger safari.

Wakeup early morning and Get ready for morning ranthambore tiger safari by 5:30am, observe the gorgeous Tigers and other wildlife that call the grounds home. Breakfast and lunch will be served at the hotel later. After Lunch you will visit most famous Lord Ganesha Temple and Ranthambore Fort. Comeback to Hotel and Overnight stay in Hotel.

On the sixth day checkout from hotel after breakfast, Drive to Agra from ranthambore, enroute You will visit Fatehpur Sikri, an abandoned town made by mughal emperor Akbar. The sightseeing tour of Fatehpur Sikri involves sights like Buland Darwaza(the biggest entryway), Jodha Bai’s Palace and Salim Chisti’s Tomb. After 0ne hour drive, you will reach Agra city and Check-in to your hotel and Overnight stay in Agra.

Wakeup early morning and Explore sunrise view of the Taj Mahal. It is best time to see Taj Mahal and You can capture some stunning photos of the Taj Mahal. After exploring Taj Mahal back to Hotel for Breakfast, After breakfast checkout from hotel and Visit one of the most famous monument Agra Fort and Explore popular local handicraft market of Agra city. Enjoy your lunch at Multi cuisine restaurant and After lunch drive to Delhi enroute you will visit Itmad-Ud-Daulah. After 3 hours drive check-inn to your pre-booked hotel in Delhi and Overnight stay in Delhi.

As per your flight schedule checkout from hotel and our representative will drop you at Delhi international airport. This tour ends here unforgettable Memories of India trip.
